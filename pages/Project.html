PUBLICATIONS

  • Two-Branch Stacked Transformer for 2D Skeleton-based Action Recognition. 17th International Conference on Ubiquitous Information Management and Communication (IMCOM 2023), Suwon, South Korea, Jan 3-5, 2023.

    • We propose a model combining short action-snippets for storing meaningful information about human body transition and a deep network configured by two parallel branches of Transformer for thoroughly learning the temporal correlation of skeletal representations in upper and lower body parts, hence concurrently enabling to handle of partial occlusions of skeleton data and boosting the HAR performance. In experiments, we validate the proposed approach's outperformance compared with the state-of-the-art methods on the JHMDB dataset in terms of both the size (i.e., number of learned parameters) and the accuracy.
