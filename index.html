Zhezkazgan

Jezkazgan or Zhezkazgan formerly known as Dzhezkazgan until 1992, is a city and the administrative centre of Ulytau Region, Kazakhstan, on a reservoir of the Kara-Kengir River.

Population: 86,227. Its urban area includes the neighbouring mining town of Satpayev, for a total city population of 148,700.

  • 55% of population are Kazakhs
  • 30% Russians
  • smaller minorities of Ukrainians, Germans, Chechens and Koreans
